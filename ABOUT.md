# About Foodie Frenzy

Foodie Frenzy was developed as a solution for modern food delivery businesses. The goal was to create a seamless experience for both the customer and the restaurant owner.

### Core Objectives:
- **Scalability**: Using a decoupled architecture (Frontend, Admin, Backend).
- **Security**: Ensuring user data and payments are handled safely.
- **Speed**: Leveraging Vite for fast development and high-performance production builds.
